## Design Context

### Users
TrendX is used by a single operator running a crypto futures trend-following system.
The operator monitors `BTCUSDT` and `ETHUSDT`, checks trend state, risk, and execution
status, and needs to make fast trust-based judgments without scanning a long page.

### Brand Personality
Disciplined, minimal, decisive.
The interface should feel calm, sharp, and institutional rather than promotional or
retail-hype driven.

### Aesthetic Direction
Primary direction: black minimal trading console.
Reference feel: KairoTrend-inspired restraint, dense operator focus, sparse accent use,
and strong contrast.
Anti-references: oversized slogans, decorative invalid metrics, long explanatory copy,
generic blue-purple glow dashboards, and stacked pages that exceed one screen.

### Design Principles
1. Keep the dashboard within a single-screen control-room rhythm whenever possible.
2. Prioritize real trading fields over decorative marketing language.
3. Use black and near-black surfaces with restrained warm neutrals and sparse signal
   accents.
4. Make hierarchy come from spacing, typography, and contrast, not heavy ornament.
5. Preserve execution clarity first; decoration only exists if it improves legibility.
